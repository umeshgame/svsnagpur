About Our School

Principal’s Desk

As I walk down the hallways of the school everyday, I can hear the exuberant chatter of very very eager minds, the shouts of excitement from the tiny tots, the thumping sound of dancer feet and melodious voices harmonizing. The energy, movement and enthusiasm which permeates the atmosphere at SANSKAR VIDYA SAGAR is an experience of a different world altogether. It gives such an immense pleasure and warmth that fills my heart with joy and peace. We are a school where we value individualism, creativity and innovation and strive to nurture them in our students. Our motto “MIND IS AN ASSET, SANSKAR JUST ENHANCES IT” explains it all. We aim to not just impart knowledge to the students, but also to inculcate in them – SANSKARA. The mind is to be taught the ability to distinguish good from evil and what is right and what is wrong. The School boasts of modern state of art infrastructure, qualified, experienced and caring staff, well equipped computer lab, composite laboratories and library. Economical fee structure, play zone area are other features. We have a multi-cultural student population; hence we teach children the importance of tolerance and respecting each other’s culture. Discipline, values and integrity are the very foundation of this school. Besides rigorous scholastic programmes, we seek to develop and nurture the different facets of a child. The school encourages all students to participate in a variety of co-curricular activities from dance, art and drama to a variety of sports to social work to environmental conservation activities, (the list goes on). It is important for a child to explore and find their strengths in order to reach their true potential. Our mission is to continue to do what we have always done: develop this school and the students with integrity and values and also, to give our students the best opportunities and the best all-round education.

-Shweta Deshpande

(Principal)

Admission & Withdrawals

Admission to the school is open to all students, subject to their fulfillment of required conditions, and the availability of seats.
Students seeking admission in primary school, will be admitted after passing test in English and Mats.
Students from other school will be required to submit their Transfer certificate from the Corporation or Nagar parishad.
Parents are required to pay the admission and other fees on or before notified date, failing which the admission of their child / ward may be cancelled.
The school reserves the right of admission. The filling of an admission/registration form is in no way a gurantee for admission . The decision of the school committee will be final. Parents has to provide a written application for T.C. and T.C. will be issued after 8 days of the date of application.
A school Leaving Certificate will be issued only after the student has paid all dues in full.
Irregular attendance,habitual lack of interest in academics, neglect of homework, disobedience and disrespect to members of the school staff, rude behavior ,non/irregular payment of school dues constitute sufficient reasons for the removal of a students from the school.
Notwithstanding anything contained in these rules, the Principal at Discretion ,may ask any parents at any parent at any time to withdraw his/ her ward from the school.
Feed onces deposited will not be refunded.

Our Aim

We believe that the foundation of a person’s character and personality are laid in the very childhood through proper upbringing. “Sanskara”, the Sanskrit word included training of the mind as well as the body. The mind is to be tought the ability to distinguish good from evil, proper from improper and noble from the base.We imbibe moral values in a child; teach him/her the importance of both mental and physical health and do our atmost to develop him/ her into an ideal citizen of tomorrow’s India.
