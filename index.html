Examination

Education does not mean passing an examination. We therefore conduct several periodic tests to access the performance and over all progress of the child. Continuous Comprehensive evaluation (CCE) formulated by CBSE is strictly followed. Both formative and Summative Assessment will be carried out throughout the year along with the periodic tests.The academic year will be from Mid June to April divided into the three terms for std. I to V & two terms for Vi to X
