Beyond the culinary delights, Millet Week at Sanskar Vidya Sagar emphasized the larger message of sustainability and conscious eating. It served as a reminder that our food choices play a crucial role in shaping not only our health but also the health of the planet. The initiative concluded with a sense of accomplishment and a deeper appreciation for the humble millet. Millet Week at Sanskar Vidya Sagar was not just a celebration of food; it was a celebration of knowledge, diversity, and a commitment to holistic well-being.

The event came to a conclusion with the appreciation for all the Sanskarians by Principal and Vote of thanks by Leena Sharma ma’am.
